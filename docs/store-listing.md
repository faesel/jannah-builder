# App Store Listing — Jannah Builder

## Short Description (80 chars)
Log your daily prayers and watch a peaceful garden grow, one day at a time.

## Full Description

Jannah Builder is a calm, spiritually-sensitive prayer tracking app that turns your daily prayers into a beautiful, growing pixel-art landscape.

**How it works:**
• Log your five daily prayers (Fajr, Dhuhr, Asr, Maghrib, Isha)
• Watch trees, flowers, and buildings appear as you stay consistent
• Mark Qur'an reading and dhikr for gentle visual enhancements
• Explore your growing world on an interactive pixel-art map

**Designed with care:**
• No guilt, no pressure — gentle encouragement only
• Missed a day? Only one tree is gently affected
• No streaks displayed, no leaderboards, no comparisons
• Seasons change with your journey — spring, summer, autumn, winter
• Beautiful temporary items appear during long periods of consistency

**Features:**
• Five daily prayer toggles with haptic feedback
• Qur'an and dhikr daily logging (simple yes/no)
• Pixel-art Jannah map that grows with your prayers
• Gentle visual effects — golden light for Qur'an, floating particles for dhikr
• Statistics and streak tracking (presented as a journey, not a competition)
• Fully offline — all data stays on your device
• No account required

**Privacy:**
This app collects no personal data. All prayer logs are stored locally on your device. No analytics, no tracking, no third-party data sharing.
