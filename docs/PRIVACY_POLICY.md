# Privacy Policy — Jannah Builder

**Last updated:** 13 May 2026

## Overview

Jannah Builder is a prayer-logging app designed for personal spiritual reflection. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the app handles and how.

## Data Collection

**Jannah Builder does not collect any personal data.**

- No account or registration is required
- No email addresses, names, or contact information are collected
- No location data is accessed
- No camera or microphone access is used
- No analytics or tracking tools are embedded
- No advertisements are displayed

## Data Storage

All data is stored **locally on your device only**:

- Prayer logs (which prayers were completed each day)
- Qur'an and dhikr daily logs (simple yes/no)
- Garden state (trees, buildings, animals, visual elements)
- App preferences

This data never leaves your device. There are no servers, no cloud sync, and no remote databases.

## Third-Party Services

Jannah Builder does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, advertising networks, or social media APIs.

## Data Sharing

**We do not share any data with anyone.** Since no data is collected or transmitted, there is nothing to share.

## Data Deletion

You can delete all app data at any time:

- Use the **Reset Garden** option in the Settings page to clear all prayer logs and garden progress
- Uninstalling the app removes all stored data from your device

## Children's Privacy

Jannah Builder does not knowingly collect data from children. The app contains no data collection mechanisms regardless of the user's age.

## Permissions

The app requests no special device permissions. It does not access your camera, microphone, contacts, location, or any other sensitive device features.

## Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the changes will be reflected in the app's repository and the "Last updated" date above will be revised.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on the [GitHub repository](https://github.com/faesel/jannah-builder).
